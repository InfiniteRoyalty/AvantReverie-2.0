About Me

My name is India and I love to draw and create things, my current obsession is digital art which I create using my Ipad, or computer (krita or photoshop). I started drawing digitally in 2020 but I’ve been drawing traditionally since I was 8 years old. Art is my passion; I have a lot of goals when it comes to art. I hope to have a freelance career involving art in some way my dream is to work for a comic company one day maybe Marvel or Dc. I don’t have a specific field I want to be in; however I was thinking either Comic Artist or Concept Artist.

I asked my brother to design this site so once my art is where I want it I can put together a more professional portfolio and start building towards an art career – so thank you to my brother for being a big support and helping me work towards my dream! (he’s also an artist too!)

Here are some things about me if any of my followers/supporters want to get to know me. My favorite animals are panda’s cats, and ferrets, I have a cat named after one of my favorite superhero’s (Storm- X Men) I never took any professional college classes for art I had art in middle school and joined an art club in high school, but I learned everything I know from books, and tutorials on YouTube. I love sushi but my favorite food is curry chicken I never left the country I went to 3 different states. I started getting back into art when the pandemic started and I decided to buy an ipad, I love horror movies Nope, US and the entire Scream franchise are some of my favorites, I have a mini comic book collection, I really need to read them more but I spend all my time drawing lol
